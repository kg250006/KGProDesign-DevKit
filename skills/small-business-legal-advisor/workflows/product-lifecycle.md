<objective>
Track products from conception through sunset with appropriate legal support at each stage.
</objective>

<required_reading>
@references/business-context.md
@references/compliance-calendar.md
</required_reading>

<process>

<step_1 name="product-intake">
Identify product and current stage:

<product_types>
- SaaS/Software
- Mobile Application
- Physical Product
- Digital Product (ebook, course)
- Service
- Marketplace/Platform
</product_types>

<product_stages>
- Planning: Concept and design phase
- Development: Building the product
- Beta: Testing with limited users
- Live: Generally available
- Growth: Scaling and expanding
- Mature: Stable, maintenance mode
- Sunset: Winding down
</product_stages>

Gather context:
- Product description
- Target users/customers
- Business model (pricing, revenue)
- Technical architecture (where data stored, integrations)
- Compliance requirements identified
- Current legal documentation
</step_1>

<step_2 name="stage-requirements">
Generate stage-specific legal requirements:

<stage name="planning">
Legal tasks:
- IP protection assessment (trademarks, patents)
- Regulatory landscape review
- Competitor analysis (IP conflicts)
- Initial contracts with co-founders/partners
- Non-disclosure agreements for discussions

Key questions:
- Who owns the IP?
- What regulations will apply?
- Are there trademark conflicts?
- What agreements are needed with partners?
</stage>

<stage name="development">
Legal tasks:
- Contractor agreements (with IP assignment)
- Employee IP assignment agreements
- Third-party license compliance (open source)
- Initial terms of service drafting
- Privacy policy drafting (if collecting data)

Key questions:
- Are all contributors bound by IP agreements?
- Is open source being used correctly?
- What data will be collected?
</stage>

<stage name="beta">
Legal tasks:
- Beta user agreement
- Privacy policy (live and complete)
- Terms of service (beta version)
- Data processing agreements (if applicable)
- Limitation of liability for beta
- Feedback collection rights

Key questions:
- How is beta different from production?
- What expectations are set with users?
- How is feedback handled?
</stage>

<stage name="live">
Legal tasks:
- Final terms of service
- Production privacy policy
- Cookie policy (if applicable)
- Compliance documentation complete
- Business associate agreements (HIPAA)
- Data processing agreements (GDPR)
- Insurance review

Key questions:
- Are all policies finalized and accessible?
- Is compliance documentation complete?
- Are vendor agreements in place?
</stage>

<stage name="growth">
Legal tasks:
- International terms consideration
- Additional data processing agreements
- Partnership/integration agreements
- Reseller/affiliate agreements
- API terms of service
- Updated privacy practices
- Trademark registration in new markets

Key questions:
- Are you expanding to new markets?
- New partnerships or integrations?
- Do terms need updating for scale?
</stage>

<stage name="mature">
Legal tasks:
- Regular policy reviews (annual)
- Contract renewals
- Compliance audits
- Intellectual property maintenance
- Risk assessment updates

Key questions:
- When were policies last updated?
- Are all filings current?
- Any new regulations affecting the product?
</stage>

<stage name="sunset">
Legal tasks:
- User notification requirements
- Data retention and deletion
- Contract wind-down
- Refund/credit policies
- Transition assistance
- Post-termination obligations
- Archive requirements

Key questions:
- What notice must be given to users?
- How will data be handled?
- What are ongoing obligations after shutdown?
</stage>

</step_2>

<step_4 name="compliance-tracking">
Link product to ongoing obligations:

<tracking_elements>
Product profile:
- Current stage
- Applicable regulations
- Key compliance dates
- Document inventory

Regular reviews:
- Quarterly: Regulation changes, policy updates
- Annual: Full compliance review, policy refresh
- Trigger-based: New markets, new features, incidents

Document management:
- Current versions of all legal documents
- Amendment history
- Acceptance records (for ToS changes)
</tracking_elements>
</step_4>

</process>

<stage_transitions>

<transition from="planning" to="development">
Before starting development:
- IP ownership clarified
- Key regulatory requirements identified
- Initial NDA/contractor agreements ready
</transition>

<transition from="development" to="beta">
Before beta launch:
- Beta agreement drafted
- Privacy policy live
- Terms of service (beta) ready
- Data collection practices documented
</transition>

<transition from="beta" to="live">
Before general availability:
- All policies finalized
- Compliance requirements met
- Insurance in place
- Vendor agreements complete
- Support processes documented
</transition>

<transition to="sunset">
Before announcing sunset:
- User notification plan
- Data handling plan
- Refund/credit policy
- Timeline for wind-down
- Post-sunset obligations identified
</transition>

</stage_transitions>
