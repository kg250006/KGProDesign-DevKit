<usage_notes>
Frequency Recommendations:
- Quarterly: Full checklist review for growing companies
- Annual: Minimum for all businesses
- Trigger-based: After major changes (funding, new states, acquisitions)

Preparation:
- Gather prior audit results
- Access Secretary of State portals
- Have insurance certificates ready
- Export current contract list

Common Findings:
- Lapsed registered agent services
- Outdated privacy policies
- Missing contractor agreements
- Expired licenses
- Inadequate documentation

Escalation:
- Legal counsel for compliance gaps
- Accountant for tax issues
- Insurance broker for coverage gaps
</usage_notes>
