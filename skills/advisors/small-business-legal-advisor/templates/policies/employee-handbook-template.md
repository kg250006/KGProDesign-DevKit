[COMPANY NAME] is committed to a workplace free from harassment and discrimination. We prohibit harassment based on any protected characteristic.

3.2 Definition of Harassment

Harassment includes unwelcome conduct based on protected characteristics that:
- Is severe or pervasive enough to create a hostile work environment, or
- Results in an adverse employment decision

Examples include:
- Offensive jokes, slurs, or name-calling
- Physical threats or assault
- Intimidation or ridicule
- Offensive pictures or objects
- Interference with work performance

3.3 Sexual Harassment

Sexual harassment includes:
- Unwelcome sexual advances
- Requests for sexual favors
- Verbal or physical conduct of a sexual nature that affects employment or creates a hostile environment

3.4 Reporting Procedure

If you experience or witness harassment:
1. Report to your manager, HR, or [DESIGNATED CONTACT]
2. You may also report to [ALTERNATIVE CONTACT / HOTLINE if applicable]
3. All reports will be taken seriously and investigated promptly
4. You will not be retaliated against for making a good-faith report

3.5 Investigation and Resolution

All complaints will be investigated fairly and confidentially to the extent possible. Employees found to have engaged in harassment will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
