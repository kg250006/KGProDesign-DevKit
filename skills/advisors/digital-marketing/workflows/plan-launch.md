Read these reference files based on launch type:
- New business: references/product-launch-playbook.md or references/service-launch-playbook.md
- Course/Program: references/course-launch-playbook.md
- SaaS/Software: references/saas-launch-playbook.md
- All launches: references/pre-launch-checklist.md

</required_reading>

<process>

<step_1 name="determine-launch-type">

Ask: What are you launching?

| Launch Type | Playbook | Key Considerations |
|-------------|----------|-------------------|
| New business | product-launch-playbook or service-launch-playbook | Brand awareness, initial customers |
| Physical product | product-launch-playbook | Inventory, fulfillment, shipping |
| Digital product | product-launch-playbook | Delivery system, access management |
| Service business | service-launch-playbook | Positioning, case studies, referrals |
| Course/Program | course-launch-playbook | Live vs evergreen, enrollment windows |
| SaaS/Software | saas-launch-playbook | Beta program, early access, retention |

Identify launch goals:
- Revenue target for launch period
- Number of customers/users
- Email list growth
- Brand awareness metrics

</step_1>

<step_2 name="pre-launch-phase">

Timeline: 30-60 days before launch

Validate offer:
- Is there proven demand?
- Have you pre-sold or gotten commitments?
- What's the pricing strategy?

Build anticipation:
- Create landing page with waitlist
- Develop teaser content series
- Engage potential customers in development

Grow email list:
- Create lead magnet related to launch
- Run awareness campaigns
- Partner with complementary businesses

Create content assets:
- Sales page/website copy
- Email sequences (pre-launch, launch, post-launch)
- Social media content calendar
- Video content (demos, testimonials)
- Press/media materials

</step_2>

<step_3 name="launch-phase">

Timeline: 7-14 days

Day-by-day launch schedule:
- Day 1: Announcement email + social blast
- Day 2-3: Share behind-the-scenes, early results
- Day 4-5: Case studies, testimonials, social proof
- Day 6-7: Objection handling, FAQ content
- Day 8-10: Urgency messaging (if applicable)
- Final days: Last chance, deadline reminders

Multi-channel coordination:
- Email sequences timed and coordinated
- Social media posting schedule
- Paid advertising (if applicable)
- Partner/affiliate promotions
- Live events (webinars, Q&As)

Active engagement:
- Respond to comments and questions quickly
- Monitor social media mentions
- Handle objections in real-time
- Celebrate wins publicly

</step_3>

<step_4 name="post-launch-phase">

Timeline: 30 days after launch

Momentum maintenance:
- Continue engagement with new customers
- Share early success stories
- Address any issues quickly
- Keep content flowing

Feedback collection:
- Survey new customers
- Conduct post-purchase interviews
- Document testimonials
- Identify improvement opportunities

Iteration planning:
- What worked well?
- What underperformed?
- What would you do differently?
- Plan for next launch or evergreen transition

</step_4>

</process>

- Team roles assigned

</category>
