<process>

<step_1 name="map-customer-journey">

Understand the full customer journey:

Awareness Stage:
- How do prospects first discover you?
- What content attracts them?
- What problem are they searching for solutions to?

Consideration Stage:
- What information do they need to evaluate options?
- What objections or concerns do they have?
- How do they compare alternatives?

Decision Stage:
- What triggers the purchase decision?
- What final reassurances do they need?
- What makes them choose you over alternatives?

Retention Stage:
- How do you keep customers engaged?
- What creates repeat purchases?
- How do you prevent churn?

Advocacy Stage:
- What motivates customers to refer others?
- How do you make sharing easy?
- What recognition do advocates receive?

</step_1>

<step_2 name="select-funnel-type">

Choose funnel based on business model and offer:

| Business Type | Recommended Funnel | Why |
|--------------|-------------------|-----|
| Service provider (low-ticket) | Lead Generation | Build list, nurture, sell |
| Service provider (high-ticket) | Application | Qualify leads before sales call |
| Course creator | Webinar | Educate and sell in one event |
| SaaS | Lead Gen + Free Trial | Demonstrate value before purchase |
| E-commerce | E-commerce + Email | Direct purchase + abandoned cart |
| Info products (low-ticket) | Tripwire | Convert leads to buyers quickly |
| Coaching/Consulting | Application | Pre-qualify serious prospects |

</step_2>

<step_3 name="design-lead-magnet">

Create compelling opt-in offer:

Lead magnet characteristics:
- Solves a specific, urgent problem
- Delivers quick win or immediate value
- Demonstrates your expertise
- Naturally leads to paid offer

Lead magnet types:
- Checklists (easy to create, high conversion)
- Templates (practical, immediately usable)
- Guides/Ebooks (in-depth, establishes authority)
- Video training (personal, builds trust)
- Quizzes (engaging, provides personalization)
- Free tools/calculators (ongoing value)
- Swipe files (proven examples to copy)

Lead magnet format:
- Title that promises specific outcome
- Clear description of what they'll learn
- Professional design or presentation
- Easy delivery (email or instant access)

</step_3>

<step_4 name="build-email-sequences">

Welcome Sequence (5-7 emails over 7-10 days):
- Email 1: Deliver lead magnet + welcome
- Email 2: Your story + why you care
- Email 3: Quick win or valuable tip
- Email 4: Social proof + testimonials
- Email 5: Soft pitch or invitation

Nurture Sequence (ongoing):
- Value-focused content
- Educational emails
- Story-based lessons
- Occasional soft promotions

Sales Sequence (5-7 emails over 7-14 days):
- Email 1: Problem agitation
- Email 2: Solution introduction
- Email 3: Case study/proof
- Email 4: Objection handling
- Email 5: Deadline/urgency
- Email 6: Last chance

</step_4>

<step_5 name="create-conversion-points">

Landing Pages:
- Lead magnet opt-in page
- Thank you / delivery page
- Sales page (short or long form)
- Checkout page
- Order confirmation

Page elements:
- Compelling headline
- Clear benefit statements
- Social proof elements
- Strong call to action
- Mobile optimization

Checkout optimization:
- Minimize form fields
- Multiple payment options
- Trust badges and security
- Clear return policy
- Order summary visible

Follow-up automation:
- Purchase confirmation
- Onboarding sequence
- Upsell/cross-sell offers
- Re-engagement for non-buyers

</step_5>

</process>

<funnel_metrics>

Key metrics to track:
- Opt-in rate (landing page visitors to subscribers)
- Email open rate (subscribers who open emails)
- Click-through rate (opens to clicks)
- Sales page conversion (visitors to purchases)
- Cart abandonment rate (carts to purchases)
- Customer lifetime value (total revenue per customer)
- Cost per acquisition (total spend to acquire customer)

Benchmark targets:
- Opt-in page: 20-40% conversion
- Email open rate: 20-30%
- Email click rate: 2-5%
- Sales page: 1-5% conversion
- Cart completion: 60-80%

</funnel_metrics>
