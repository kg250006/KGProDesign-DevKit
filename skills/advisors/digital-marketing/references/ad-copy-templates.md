<overview>
Advertising copy templates and frameworks for Facebook, Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn ads.
</overview>

<ad_copy_principles>

<principle name="hook-first">
First line must stop the scroll. Lead with curiosity, controversy, or clear benefit.
</principle>

<principle name="speak-to-one">
Write as if speaking to one specific person, not a crowd.
</principle>

<principle name="benefit-over-feature">
Focus on what they get, not what it is.
</principle>

<principle name="clear-cta">
Tell them exactly what to do next.
</principle>

<principle name="match-intent">
Cold audience needs education. Warm audience needs offers.
</principle>

</ad_copy_principles>

<hook_formulas>

<category name="curiosity">
- "I can't believe this actually worked..."
- "Nobody talks about this, but..."
- "The #1 thing [experts] won't tell you about [topic]"
- "I was today years old when I learned..."
- "Plot twist: [Unexpected statement]"
</category>

<category name="controversy">
- "Unpopular opinion: [Bold statement]"
- "[Common advice] is destroying your [area]"
- "I stopped [common practice] and here's what happened"
- "Why I'll never [common action] again"
</category>

<category name="benefit">
- "How I [achieved result] in [timeframe]"
- "The fastest way to [outcome]"
- "[Number] people learned this. Now they [result]."
- "Get [outcome] without [pain]"
</category>

<category name="story">
- "6 months ago, I was [situation]..."
- "I almost gave up on [goal]. Then..."
- "My biggest failure taught me..."
- "The day everything changed was..."
</category>

<category name="question">
- "Are you making this mistake with [topic]?"
- "What if you could [dream outcome]?"
- "Tired of [common frustration]?"
- "Why does [problem] keep happening?"
</category>

<category name="callout">
- "Attention [specific audience]:"
- "If you're a [role] who [situation]..."
- "This is for you if [qualifier]"
- "[Specific group] - read this carefully"
</category>

</hook_formulas>
