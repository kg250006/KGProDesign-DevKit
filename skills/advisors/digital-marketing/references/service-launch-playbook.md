<overview>
Service business launch methodology for consultants, coaches, agencies, and professional service providers.
</overview>

<service_launch_phases>

<phase name="foundation" timeline="30+ days before">

<task_group name="service-packaging-pricing">
Define your service offering:
- Core service description (what you do)
- Target client (who it's for)
- Transformation promise (what they get)
- Delivery method (how you deliver)
- Timeline (how long it takes)

Package options:
- Done-for-you (you do the work)
- Done-with-you (collaboration)
- Done-by-you (training/coaching)
- Hybrid (combination)

Pricing strategy:
- Value-based (outcome-focused)
- Project-based (fixed scope)
- Retainer (ongoing relationship)
- Hourly (time-based - use sparingly)
</task_group>

<task_group name="case-study-development">
Create proof of results:
- Document past client successes
- Gather specific metrics and outcomes
- Write compelling before/after stories
- Collect video testimonials if possible
- Create portfolio of work samples
</task_group>

<task_group name="personal-brand-building">
Establish authority:
- Define your unique point of view
- Clarify your methodology or framework
- Create thought leadership content
- Optimize LinkedIn profile
- Develop professional bio and headshot
</task_group>

<task_group name="authority-content-creation">
Build credibility through content:
- Write pillar blog posts
- Create lead magnet demonstrating expertise
- Develop webinar or workshop
- Pitch podcast appearances
- Seek speaking opportunities
</task_group>

</phase>

<phase name="lead-generation" timeline="ongoing">

<task_group name="lead-magnet-creation">
Value-first opt-in offers:
- Free assessment or audit
- Template or toolkit
- Mini-course or training
- Checklist or framework
- Industry report or guide
</task_group>

<task_group name="consultation-setup">
Design discovery call process:
- Booking system (Calendly, etc.)
- Pre-call questionnaire
- Call structure and script
- Follow-up process
- Proposal template
</task_group>

<task_group name="referral-program">
Leverage existing relationships:
- Formalize referral request process
- Create referral incentives
- Partner with complementary providers
- Ask for introductions systematically
- Thank and recognize referral sources
</task_group>

<task_group name="speaking-guest-content">
Borrow other people's audiences:
- Pitch podcast interviews
- Write guest blog posts
- Speak at industry events
- Host webinars for partners
- Participate in expert roundups
</task_group>

</phase>

<service_specific_tactics>

<tactic name="authority-building">
Consultants and coaches must establish expertise:
- Develop proprietary framework or methodology
- Name your process (The [X] Method)
- Create certification or training program
- Write book or comprehensive guide
- Build thought leadership platform
</tactic>

<tactic name="niching-down">
Specialization increases perceived value:
- Industry vertical (healthcare, SaaS, etc.)
- Functional specialty (SEO, leadership, etc.)
- Problem focus (scaling, turnaround, etc.)
- Client type (solopreneurs, enterprises, etc.)
</tactic>

<tactic name="productizing-services">
Make services feel like products:
- Fixed scope with clear deliverables
- Defined timeline and milestones
- Standardized pricing
- Named offerings
- Clear outcomes
</tactic>

<tactic name="retainer-transition">
Move from project to recurring:
- Identify ongoing needs clients have
- Create monthly maintenance packages
- Offer priority access or support
- Build dependency through systems you create
- Propose long-term partnerships
</tactic>

</service_specific_tactics>

<pricing_guide>

<approach name="value-based">
Price based on outcome value:
- What result is worth to client
- ROI client will achieve
- Cost of not solving problem
- Premium for transformation

Example: Leadership coach charging $25K because promotion they help secure means $50K+ salary increase.
</approach>

<approach name="project-based">
Fixed price for defined scope:
- Clear deliverables
- Defined timeline
- Scope boundaries
- Change order process

Example: Website redesign at $15K for defined pages and features.
</approach>

<approach name="retainer">
Ongoing relationship:
- Monthly fee for access/hours
- Predictable revenue
- Deeper relationships
- Long-term commitment

Example: Marketing strategy retainer at $5K/month for 10 hours and unlimited email.
</approach>

</pricing_guide>

<discovery_call_structure>

<phase time="5 min" name="rapport">
Build connection:
- Warm greeting
- How they heard about you
- Light personal connection
</phase>

<phase time="15 min" name="discovery">
Understand their situation:
- Current state and challenges
- Goals and desired outcomes
- Timeline and urgency
- Previous attempts and results
- Budget considerations
</phase>

<phase time="10 min" name="prescription">
Present your recommendation:
- Diagnose their core problem
- Explain your approach
- Share relevant examples
- Outline proposed solution
</phase>

<phase time="10 min" name="close">
Move to next step:
- Summarize understanding
- Present investment
- Address questions
- Propose next step
- Set deadline for decision
</phase>

</discovery_call_structure>
