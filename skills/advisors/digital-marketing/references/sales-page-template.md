<overview>
Complete sales page template with section-by-section guidance for high-converting sales pages.
</overview>

<full_template>

<section_1 name="pre-headline">
Purpose: Call out your audience

Format: "Attention [specific audience]:" or "For [audience] who [situation]"

Examples:
- "Attention Course Creators Who Want More Students"
- "For Coaches Who Are Tired of Chasing Clients"
- "If You're a Service Provider Making Under $10K/Month..."
</section_1>

<section_2 name="headline">
Purpose: Big promise that stops them

Formula: [Achieve Outcome] + [Timeframe/Method] + [Without Pain]

Examples:
- "Launch Your First Profitable Course in 30 Days (Even If You've Never Created One Before)"
- "Get 10 High-Ticket Clients Every Month Without Cold Calling or Expensive Ads"
- "Double Your Email Revenue in 90 Days Using the 'Triple A' System"
</section_2>

<section_3 name="subheadline">
Purpose: Expand on headline, add intrigue

Formula: Elaborate on the promise or add specificity

Examples:
- "The step-by-step system that's helped 500+ creators build 6-figure course businesses"
- "Discover the exact client-attraction method I've used to generate $2.3M in coaching revenue"
</section_3>

<section_4 name="opening-hook">
Purpose: Connect emotionally, start the story

Template:
If you're [experiencing problem], then this might be the most important page you ever read.

Because I'm about to show you [specific benefit] that will change how you [area of life/business].

But first, let me ask you something...

Are you tired of [frustration 1]?
Are you sick of [frustration 2]?
Are you ready for [positive change]?

If you said yes to any of these, keep reading...
</section_4>

<section_5 name="problem-identification">
Purpose: Show you understand their pain

Template:
Here's what most [audience] deal with every day:

You [problem 1]. You spend hours on [ineffective activity] but see little results.

You've tried [failed solution 1], but it didn't work because [reason].

You've tried [failed solution 2], but that failed too because [reason].

And every day you're not [achieving goal], it costs you [specific cost - money, time, stress].

Sound familiar?

It's not your fault...
</section_5>

<section_6 name="bridge">
Purpose: Transition from problem to solution

Template:
The reason nothing has worked is because you've been missing [key insight].

You see, most advice tells you to [common advice]. But that approach is fundamentally flawed.

Here's why: [Explanation of why common approaches fail]

The truth is, [real insight].

And once you understand this, everything changes.

That's exactly what I'm going to show you today.
</section_6>

<section_7 name="credentials">
Purpose: Establish your authority

Template:
My name is [Your Name], and I've [impressive credential/result].

Over the past [timeframe], I've:
- [Achievement 1]
- [Achievement 2]
- [Achievement 3]

I've helped [number] [type of clients] achieve [result].

[Brief story of how you discovered this approach]

And now I want to share everything I've learned with you.
</section_7>

<section_8 name="product-introduction">
Purpose: Reveal what you're offering

Template:
Introducing [Product Name]:

The [description] that shows you exactly how to [main outcome].

This isn't just another [type of product that gets ignored].

This is a complete [system/program/course] that takes you from [starting point] to [ending point].

Inside, you'll discover:
- [Key learning 1]
- [Key learning 2]
- [Key learning 3]
</section_8>

<section_9 name="module-breakdown">
Purpose: Show what's included with benefits

Template:
Here's Everything You Get Inside [Product Name]:

MODULE 1: [Name] - [Subtitle]
In this module, you'll discover [what they learn]. By the end, you'll be able to [capability]. This means [benefit]. (Value: $XXX)

MODULE 2: [Name] - [Subtitle]
In this module, you'll master [skill]. You'll learn [specific things]. This gives you [outcome]. (Value: $XXX)

[Continue for all modules...]
</section_9>

BONUS #1: [Name] (Value: $XXX)
[Description of bonus and why it's valuable]

BONUS #3: [Name] (Value: $XXX)
[Description of bonus and why it's valuable]

</section_10>

Purpose: Provide social proof

Template:

- [Name], [Title/Location]

Template:

<section_13 name="price-reveal">
Purpose: Present the investment

Template:
Your investment today is just [Price].

That's [breakdown if helpful - "less than $X per day for the next year"].

Think about it:
- You'd pay [higher price] for [alternative that's not as good]

</section_13>

<section_14 name="guarantee">
Purpose: Remove risk

Template:
Your Investment is Protected by My [X-Day] Money-Back Guarantee

I'm so confident that [Product Name] will [benefit] that I'm willing to take all the risk.

Try it for [X] days. Go through the modules. Implement what you learn.

If you don't [specific result] or you're not completely satisfied for any reason, just email us and we'll refund every penny. No questions asked.

You literally have nothing to lose and everything to gain.
</section_14>

<section_15 name="urgency">
Purpose: Drive action now

Template:

Only [number] spots available at this price. Once they're gone, enrollment closes.
