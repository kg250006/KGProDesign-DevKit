<overview>
Comprehensive pre-launch preparation checklist covering all essential elements before any launch.
</overview>

<checklists>

<section name="offer-validation">

<item status="required">Problem validated with real customers</item>
<verification>Conducted at least 10 customer interviews confirming pain point</verification>

<item status="required">Pricing tested/validated</item>
<verification>Pre-orders, surveys, or competitor analysis confirms price point</verification>

<item status="required">Unique value proposition clear</item>
<verification>Can articulate UVP in one sentence; tested with target audience</verification>

<item status="required">Competitive differentiation defined</item>
<verification>Clear answer to "Why choose us over alternatives?"</verification>

<item status="recommended">Early customers or beta testers acquired</item>
<verification>Have testimonials or case studies from real users</verification>

<item status="recommended">Refund/return policy determined</item>
<verification>Policy written and legally reviewed</verification>

</section>

<section name="marketing-assets">

<item status="required">Landing page live and tested</item>
<verification>Page loads fast, forms work, tracking installed, mobile-responsive</verification>

<item status="required">Lead magnet created</item>
<verification>Valuable content ready, delivery automated, opt-in tested</verification>

<item status="required">Email sequences written and loaded</item>
<verification>Welcome, pre-launch, launch, and post-purchase sequences ready</verification>

<item status="required">Social media content prepared</item>
<verification>2+ weeks of content scheduled, images sized correctly</verification>

<item status="required">Sales page/offer page complete</item>
<verification>Copy finalized, testimonials added, checkout tested</verification>

<item status="recommended">Ad creative ready (if using paid)</item>
<verification>Images/videos created, copy written, audiences defined</verification>

<item status="recommended">Press kit/media assets created</item>
<verification>Logo files, product images, key facts, press release ready</verification>

<item status="recommended">Affiliate/partner materials ready</item>
<verification>Swipe copy, images, tracking links, terms documented</verification>

</section>

<section name="technical-setup">

<item status="required">Email platform configured and tested</item>
<verification>Deliverability tested, segments created, automations active</verification>

<item status="required">Payment processing tested end-to-end</item>
<verification>Test purchases completed, refunds work, receipts send</verification>

<item status="required">Analytics tracking installed</item>
<verification>Google Analytics, conversion tracking, UTM strategy documented</verification>

<item status="required">Automation workflows active</item>
<verification>All trigger points tested, integrations working</verification>

<item status="required">Product delivery system tested</item>
<verification>Customers can access what they purchased immediately</verification>

<item status="recommended">A/B testing tools set up</item>
<verification>Landing page variants ready if testing</verification>

<item status="recommended">Chat/support system ready</item>
<verification>Live chat or help desk configured for launch traffic</verification>

<item status="recommended">Backup/redundancy in place</item>
<verification>Critical systems have failover, know who to call if down</verification>

</section>

<section name="launch-coordination">

<item status="required">Launch calendar created</item>
<verification>Day-by-day schedule with specific tasks and times</verification>

<item status="required">Team roles assigned</item>
<verification>Everyone knows their responsibilities for launch</verification>

<item status="required">Communication plan ready</item>
<verification>How team will coordinate during launch (Slack channel, etc.)</verification>

<item status="required">Contingency plans documented</item>
<verification>What to do if site goes down, emails don't send, etc.</verification>

<item status="required">Launch metrics dashboard created</item>
<verification>Real-time visibility into key metrics during launch</verification>

<item status="recommended">VIP/affiliate coordination complete</item>
<verification>Partners know launch timeline and have materials</verification>

<item status="recommended">Customer support prepared</item>
<verification>FAQs ready, support team briefed on common questions</verification>

</section>

<section name="legal-compliance">

<item status="required">Terms and conditions published</item>
<verification>Link in checkout, covers refund policy, usage terms</verification>

<item status="required">Privacy policy updated</item>
<verification>Reflects data collection practices, GDPR compliant if applicable</verification>

<item status="required">Refund policy clearly stated</item>
<verification>Visible on sales page and in checkout</verification>

<item status="required">Disclosure requirements met</item>
<verification>Affiliate disclosures, earnings disclaimers where needed</verification>

<item status="recommended">Copyright and IP protected</item>
<verification>Content protected, trademarks filed if applicable</verification>

<item status="recommended">Tax collection configured</item>
<verification>Sales tax/VAT set up correctly for target markets</verification>

</section>

</checklists>

<common_mistakes>

<mistake name="launching-without-list">
Problem: No audience to launch to
Solution: Build email list for 30-90 days before launch
</mistake>

<mistake name="untested-tech">
Problem: Payment or delivery breaks on launch day
Solution: Complete test purchase 48 hours before launch
</mistake>

<mistake name="unclear-offer">
Problem: Audience doesn't understand what they're getting
Solution: Have 3-5 people outside your bubble review offer
</mistake>

<mistake name="no-urgency">
Problem: People interested but don't buy
Solution: Create genuine deadline or limited availability
</mistake>

<mistake name="launch-and-disappear">
Problem: One email, then silence
Solution: Plan 5-7 email sequence over launch period
</mistake>

<mistake name="no-backup-plan">
Problem: Something goes wrong, panic ensues
Solution: Document contingency plans for likely issues
</mistake>

</common_mistakes>
