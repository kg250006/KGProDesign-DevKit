<best_practices>

<practice name="base-on-research">
Base avatars on real customer research, not assumptions. Interview existing customers, survey your email list, analyze customer support conversations.
</practice>

<practice name="create-multiple">
Create 2-3 avatars for different segments if you serve distinct customer types. But don't create so many that messaging becomes unfocused.
</practice>

<practice name="update-regularly">
Update avatars quarterly based on customer feedback, market changes, and what you learn from marketing performance.
</practice>

<practice name="use-in-marketing">
Reference your avatar when writing any marketing copy. Ask "Would [Avatar Name] respond to this?"
</practice>

<practice name="share-with-team">
Make sure everyone on the team knows the avatars. Post them where the team can see them.
</practice>

</best_practices>
