<overview>
Landing page copywriting frameworks and templates for opt-in pages, sales pages, and webinar registrations.
</overview>

<page_types>

<type name="opt-in-page">
Purpose: Capture email addresses
Elements: Headline, subhead, bullets, form, CTA
Length: Short (1 screen)
</type>

<type name="sales-page-short">
Purpose: Sell low-ticket items ($50-200)
Elements: Hero, problem, solution, offer, CTA
Length: 500-1500 words
</type>

<type name="sales-page-long">
Purpose: Sell high-ticket items ($500+)
Elements: Full persuasion sequence
Length: 3000-10000 words
</type>

<type name="webinar-registration">
Purpose: Get webinar signups
Elements: Event details, benefits, host bio, form
Length: Short-medium
</type>

</page_types>

<opt_in_page_template>

<section name="headline">
Formula: Promise specific outcome

Examples:
- "The 5-Step System to [Achieve Result]"
- "Free Guide: How to [Benefit] in [Timeframe]"
- "Discover the Secrets to [Outcome]"
- "[Number] Ways to [Solve Problem]"
</section>

<section name="subheadline">
Formula: Expand on headline or specify audience

Examples:
- "For [audience] who want [outcome]"
- "Even if [objection]"
- "Without [common pain]"
</section>

<section name="bullets">
Formula: Outcome-focused benefits (3-5)

Structure:
- "Learn [specific thing] that [benefit]"
- "Discover [secret/method] to [result]"
- "Get [deliverable] worth $[value]"

Example:
- Learn the exact template I use to write emails that get 40%+ open rates
- Discover the 3 subject line formulas that work in any industry
- Get 10 plug-and-play email templates you can use today
</section>

<section name="form">
Fields: Email only (highest conversion) or Name + Email
Button: Action-oriented ("Get Free Access" not "Submit")
</section>

<section name="social-proof">
Options:
- Number of subscribers ("Join 15,000+ marketers")
- Testimonial ("This guide changed how I approach email" - [Name])
- Trust logos ("As seen in...")
</section>

</opt_in_page_template>

<short_sales_page_template>

<section name="hero">
Headline: Big promise or transformation
Subhead: Who it's for and what it is
Visual: Product image or demonstration
CTA: Primary call to action
</section>

<section name="problem">
Describe current pain:
- What they're struggling with
- Why it's frustrating
- What they've tried that didn't work
- What happens if nothing changes
</section>

<section name="solution">
Introduce your answer:
- What it is
- How it works
- Why it's different
- What makes it work
</section>

<section name="offer">
What they get:
- Core product description
- Key features → benefits
- Bonuses if applicable
- Price and comparison to value
</section>

<section name="proof">
Why they should trust:
- Testimonials
- Results and numbers
- Your credentials
- Guarantee
</section>

<section name="cta">
Final call to action:
- Clear button
- What happens next
- Urgency if applicable
</section>

</short_sales_page_template>

<long_sales_page_template>

<section name="1-opening">
Purpose: Capture attention, create intrigue

Elements:
- Pre-headline (call out audience)
- Main headline (big promise)
- Subheadline (expand or qualify)
- Opening hook (story or problem)
</section>

<section name="2-problem-agitation">
Purpose: Connect with their pain

Elements:
- Describe the problem vividly
- Show you understand their struggle
- Agitate consequences of not solving
- List failed solutions they've tried
</section>

<section name="3-story-credibility">
Purpose: Build trust and connection

Elements:
- Your story (struggle to success)
- How you discovered the solution
- Why you're qualified to help
- What you've accomplished
</section>

<section name="4-solution-introduction">
Purpose: Present the answer

Elements:
- The big idea or unique mechanism
- Why this approach works
- What makes it different
- The transformation it creates
</section>

<section name="5-product-reveal">
Purpose: Introduce your offer

Elements:
- Product name and description
- What's included (modules, features)
- How it's delivered
- What the experience is like
</section>

<section name="6-benefits-breakdown">
Purpose: Show value for each component

Elements:
- Feature → Benefit → Outcome for each item
- What they'll learn/achieve
- How it changes their situation
</section>

<section name="7-bonuses">
Purpose: Add value and urgency

Elements:
- Bonus 1: Name, description, value
- Bonus 2: Name, description, value
- Bonus 3: Name, description, value
- Why you're including them
</section>

<section name="8-social-proof">
Purpose: Prove it works

Elements:
- Detailed testimonials
- Case studies with specific results
- Before/after transformations
- Screenshots, photos, videos
</section>

<section name="9-value-stack">
Purpose: Justify the price

Elements:
- List everything with values
- Total value: $X,XXX
- Your investment: $XXX
- Savings emphasized
</section>

<section name="10-guarantee">
Purpose: Remove risk

Elements:
- Type of guarantee (30-day, results-based)
- What they can expect
- How refund works
- Confidence statement
</section>

<section name="11-urgency-scarcity">
Purpose: Drive action now

Elements:
- Limited time (deadline)
- Limited quantity (if true)
- Price increase (if planned)
- Bonus removal (if applicable)
</section>

<section name="12-final-cta">
Purpose: Get the click

Elements:
- Recap of transformation
- Clear call to action
- What happens after purchase
- Final confidence boost
</section>

<section name="13-faq">
Purpose: Handle remaining objections

Elements:
- Common questions answered
- Objections addressed
- Final details clarified
</section>

<section name="14-ps">
Purpose: Catch skimmers

Elements:
- Restate main benefit
- Remind of deadline/urgency
- Final CTA
</section>

</long_sales_page_template>
