<platform name="linkedin">
Best for: B2B, professional services, thought leadership

Content types that work:
- Personal stories and lessons learned
- Industry insights and commentary
- How-to posts with actionable tips
- Career/business milestones
- Document carousels
- Native video

Algorithm factors:
- Dwell time (time spent reading)
- Early engagement (first hour critical)
- Comments over likes
- Posting consistency
- Profile strength

Best practices:
- Post 3-5x per week
- Best times: 7-8 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM weekdays
- Write hook in first 2 lines
- End with question or CTA
- Reply to all comments within 1 hour
- Engage on others' posts before/after posting
- Use 3-5 hashtags (mix of sizes)

Content format:
Line 1: Hook (attention-grabbing statement)
Lines 2-10: Story or insight
Final line: CTA or question
...See more (expand for longer posts)
</platform>

<platform name="instagram">
Best for: Visual brands, lifestyle, B2C, personal brands

Content types that work:
- Reels (short video, highest reach)
- Carousels (educational, high save rate)
- Stories (engagement, behind-scenes)
- Feed posts (brand aesthetic)
- Lives (real-time connection)

Algorithm factors:
- Relationship (engagement history)
- Interest (content relevance)
- Timeliness (recency)
- Saves and shares weighted heavily
- Watch time on Reels

Best practices:
- Post 1-2x daily
- Stories 3-7x daily
- Reels 3-5x per week
- Best times vary (test your audience)
- Use 5-10 relevant hashtags
- Engage 15 min before/after posting
- Respond to DMs and comments quickly

Reels strategy:
- First 3 seconds: Hook
- Pattern interrupt (text, movement)
- Trending audio when relevant
- 15-30 seconds optimal length
- Strong CTA at end
</platform>

<platform name="tiktok">
Best for: Younger audience, entertainment, trends, virality

Content types that work:
- Trending audio/challenges
- Educational content (quick tips)
- Behind-the-scenes
- Day-in-the-life
- How-to tutorials
- Relatable humor

Algorithm factors:
- Watch time and completion rate
- Replays
- Shares
- Comments
- Not follower-dependent

Best practices:
- Post 1-3x daily
- Jump on trends quickly
- First second is crucial
- Vertical format only
- Native editing preferred
- Add captions (many watch muted)
- Use trending sounds

Content formula:
- Hook (first 1-3 seconds)
- Body (main content)
- Loop (encourage rewatch)
- CTA (follow for more)
</platform>

<platform name="twitter-x">
Best for: Tech, news, thought leadership, real-time engagement

Content types that work:
- Hot takes and opinions
- Threads (educational deep-dives)
- Quick tips
- Industry commentary
- Memes and humor
- Engagement posts (questions, polls)

Algorithm factors:
- Engagement velocity
- Reply and retweet weight
- Accounts you interact with
- Recency

Best practices:
- Tweet 3-10x daily
- Best times: 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM
- Threads: number each tweet
- Engage in conversations
- Quote tweet with commentary
- Use 1-2 hashtags max

Thread structure:
1. Hook tweet (compelling first line)
2-9. Content (one idea per tweet)
10. Summary + CTA (follow, RT)
</platform>

<platform name="youtube">
Best for: Education, tutorials, long-form content, SEO

Content types that work:
- How-to tutorials
- Listicles and rankings
- Reviews and comparisons
- Vlogs and behind-scenes
- Interviews and podcasts
- Educational courses

Algorithm factors:
- Click-through rate (thumbnail + title)
- Watch time (total and percentage)
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Session time (do they watch more?)
- Subscriber conversion

Best practices:
- Post 1-4x per week consistently
- Thumbnail: Face + emotion + contrast
- Title: Keyword + curiosity/benefit
- First 30 seconds: Hook + preview
- 8-15 min optimal for ad revenue
- End screens and cards for more views
- Community tab for engagement

Video structure:
- Hook (first 15 seconds)
- Intro (brief, subscribe prompt)
- Body (numbered sections)
- CTA (like, subscribe, watch next)
</platform>

<platform name="facebook">
Best for: Local business, communities, groups, older demographic

Content types that work:
- Video (native, not links)
- Groups and community
- Events
- Live video
- Stories
- Carousel posts

Algorithm factors:
- Meaningful interactions
- Comments (especially long ones)
- Shares
- Time spent on content
- Group participation

Best practices:
- Post 1-2x daily to page
- Engage in Groups consistently
- Best times: 1-4 PM weekdays
- Native video outperforms links
- Boost top-performing organic posts
- Build and nurture Facebook Groups

Groups strategy:
- Create community around topic
- Post discussion starters
- Welcome new members
- Go live regularly
- Moderate actively
</platform>

<engagement_tactics>

<tactic name="comment-first">
Engage on others' posts before posting yours. Algorithm favors active accounts.
</tactic>

<tactic name="respond-quickly">
Reply to comments within first hour. Signals active engagement to algorithm.
</tactic>

<tactic name="ask-questions">
End posts with questions. Drives comments, boosts reach.
</tactic>

<tactic name="save-worthy">
Create content people want to save for later. High save rate = more reach.
</tactic>

<tactic name="share-worthy">
Make content easy to share. Quote graphics, helpful tips, relatable content.
</tactic>
