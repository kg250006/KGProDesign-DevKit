<overview>
Application funnel for selling high-ticket services by qualifying leads before sales conversations.
</overview>

<concept>

<definition>
An application funnel pre-qualifies prospects for high-ticket offers ($2,000+) through an application process. Only qualified leads get on sales calls, saving time and increasing close rates.
</definition>

<when_to_use>
- High-ticket services or programs ($2,000+)
- Limited capacity (can't serve everyone)
- Customized solutions requiring discovery
- Complex sales requiring conversation
</when_to_use>

</concept>

<funnel_structure>

<stage name="awareness">
Goal: Attract ideal prospects

Components:
- Content marketing
- Ads to cold audiences
- Speaking and visibility
- Referrals

Key metric: Quality traffic
</stage>

<stage name="opt-in">
Goal: Capture leads with value-first content

Components:
- Lead magnet or training
- Email capture
- Segmentation

Key metric: Opt-in rate
</stage>

<stage name="nurture">
Goal: Build relationship and demonstrate value

Components:
- Email sequence
- Long-form content
- Case studies
- Webinar or training

Key metric: Engagement and trust
</stage>

<stage name="application">
Goal: Qualify prospects

Components:
- Application page
- Qualification questions
- Booking integration

Key metric: Application completion rate
</stage>

<stage name="sales-call">
Goal: Enroll qualified prospects

Components:
- Discovery/consultation call
- Proposal presentation
- Objection handling
- Close

Key metric: Close rate (target: 30-50%)
</stage>

</funnel_structure>

<application_page>

<elements>
Headline: Speak to transformation
"Apply to Work With [Your Name/Company]"
"Application: [Program Name]"

Description: What the opportunity is
- Who this is for
- What they'll achieve
- Why you're selective

Credibility: Why you're worth applying to
- Results you've achieved
- Client testimonials
- Your unique approach

Application form: Qualification questions
- Basic info (name, email, phone)
- Business details (revenue, size)
- Current situation questions
- Goal questions
- Commitment questions
- How did you hear about us?

CTA: Clear next step
"Submit Your Application"
"Apply Now"
</elements>

<qualification_questions>
Purpose: Screen for fit before call

Business qualification:
- "What is your current monthly/annual revenue?"
- "How long have you been in business?"
- "What industry are you in?"

Problem qualification:
- "What is your biggest challenge right now?"
- "What have you already tried to solve this?"
- "What's this problem costing you?"

Goal qualification:
- "What specific outcome are you looking for?"
- "What would success look like in 6 months?"
- "Why now? What's making this urgent?"

Commitment qualification:
- "Are you ready to invest $X-Y in solving this?"
- "Are you prepared to implement and take action?"
- "Can you commit X hours/week to this?"

Red flag questions:
- Looking for magic bullet with no work
- Not able to invest (financially or time)
- Unrealistic expectations
</qualification_questions>

</application_page>

<call_booking>

<structure>
After application submitted:
1. Confirmation page with calendar embed
2. Email confirmation with prep materials
3. Reminder sequence before call

Calendar tools:
- Calendly
- Acuity
- HubSpot Meetings
- YouCanBook.me

Settings:
- Buffer between calls
- Working hours only
- Timezone detection
- Integration with CRM
</structure>

<pre_call_sequence>
Email 1 (Immediate): Application received
- Acknowledge application
- What to expect next
- Booking link if not done

Email 2 (After booking): Confirmation
- Date/time confirmation
- What to prepare
- What you'll cover
- How long it will take

Email 3 (24 hours before): Reminder
- Quick reminder
- Prep checklist
- Reschedule link

Email 4 (1 hour before): Final reminder
- Starting soon
- Zoom/phone link
- Be ready prompt
</pre_call_sequence>

</call_booking>

<sales_call_structure>

<phase name="rapport" time="5 min">
Build connection:
- Warm greeting
- Light personal connection
- Set expectations for call
</phase>

<phase name="discovery" time="20 min">
Understand their situation:
- Review application answers
- Dig deeper on challenges
- Understand goals and timeline
- Uncover emotional drivers
- Assess commitment level

Key questions:
- "Tell me more about [challenge from application]..."
- "What's this costing you right now?"
- "What happens if you don't fix this?"
- "What would solving this mean for you?"
</phase>

<phase name="prescription" time="10 min">
Present your recommendation:
- Summarize their situation
- Explain your approach
- Show how you solve their specific problem
- Share relevant case study

Transition:
"Based on what you've shared, I think I can help. Let me explain how..."
</phase>

<phase name="pitch" time="10 min">
Present your offer:
- Program/service description
- What's included
- Timeline and process
- Investment (price)
- Guarantee if applicable
</phase>

<phase name="objection-handling" time="10 min">
Address concerns:

Common objections:
- "I need to think about it" → Understand what they need to think about
- "It's too expensive" → Reframe as investment, compare to cost of problem
- "I'm not sure it will work for me" → Share relevant testimonial, offer guarantee
- "I need to talk to [spouse/partner]" → Offer to include them, schedule follow-up
- "Now isn't the right time" → Explore what would make it right, cost of waiting
</phase>

<phase name="close" time="5 min">
Get the decision:
- Direct ask: "Are you ready to move forward?"
- Collect payment or next step
- Onboarding expectations
- Celebrate the decision
</phase>

</sales_call_structure>

<follow_up>

<for_no_decision>
If they don't buy on call:
- Thank you email same day
- Follow-up email day 2-3
- Final outreach day 5-7
- Add to long-term nurture if still no
</for_no_decision>

<for_new_clients>
After enrollment:
- Welcome email with next steps
- Onboarding sequence
- Kickoff scheduling
- Access and materials
</for_new_clients>

</follow_up>

<metrics>

<metric name="application-rate">
Formula: Applications / Landing page visitors
Benchmark: 5-15%
Improve: Better positioning, clearer qualification
</metric>

<metric name="show-rate">
Formula: Calls completed / Calls booked
Benchmark: 70-85%
Improve: Better reminders, pre-call engagement
</metric>

<metric name="close-rate">
Formula: Enrollments / Calls completed
Benchmark: 20-40%
Improve: Better qualification, sales skills
</metric>

<metric name="cost-per-enrollment">
Formula: Total cost / Enrollments
Track: For scaling decisions
</metric>

</metrics>
