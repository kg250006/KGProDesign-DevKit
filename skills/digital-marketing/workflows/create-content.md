<process>

<step_1 name="define-content-pillars">

Identify 3-5 core themes:
- What topics demonstrate your expertise?
- What does your audience care most about?
- What differentiates you from competitors?
- What aligns with your value proposition?

Content pillar examples by business type:
- Marketing agency: Strategy, Tactics, Tools, Case Studies, Industry Trends
- SaaS: Product Tips, Use Cases, Integrations, Customer Success, Industry News
- Coach: Mindset, Skills, Systems, Inspiration, Results
- E-commerce: Product Features, Lifestyle, Behind-the-Scenes, Customer Stories

Map pillars to customer journey:
- Awareness: Educational, problem-focused content
- Consideration: Solution-focused, comparison content
- Decision: Proof, testimonials, specific benefits
- Retention: Advanced tips, community content
- Advocacy: Shareable, remarkable content

</step_1>

<step_2 name="select-distribution-channels">

Primary channel selection:
- Where does your audience spend most time?
- What format does your content naturally fit?
- What resources do you have consistently?

Channel characteristics:

| Channel | Best For | Content Type | Effort Level |
|---------|----------|--------------|--------------|
| Blog/SEO | Long-term traffic, authority | Long-form articles | High |
| Email | Nurturing, sales | Newsletters, sequences | Medium |
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional services | Text posts, articles | Medium |
| Instagram | Visual brands, lifestyle | Images, Stories, Reels | High |
| YouTube | Education, tutorials | Long-form video | High |
| TikTok | Younger audiences, entertainment | Short video | High |
| Podcast | Thought leadership | Audio interviews, solo | High |
| Twitter/X | Tech, news, real-time | Short updates, threads | Medium |

Repurposing strategy:
- Create one "pillar" piece of content
- Break into smaller pieces for each channel
- Example: Podcast episode → Blog post → 5 social posts → Email newsletter

</step_2>

<step_3 name="create-editorial-calendar">

Content planning structure:

Monthly themes:
- Align with business goals and seasons
- Connect to product launches or promotions
- Address seasonal audience needs

Weekly rhythm:
- Define publishing frequency per channel
- Assign content types to specific days
- Build in batch creation time

Content mix:
- 40% Educational (how-to, tips, guides)
- 30% Engaging (stories, behind-scenes, interactive)
- 20% Promotional (offers, products, CTAs)
- 10% Curated (industry news, others' content)

Calendar template:
| Week | Theme | Blog | Email | Social | Video |
|------|-------|------|-------|--------|-------|
| 1 | [Topic] | Post title | Newsletter | 5 posts | Tutorial |
| 2 | [Topic] | ... | ... | ... | ... |

</step_3>

<step_4 name="campaign-planning">

Campaign structure:

Campaign objectives:
- What specific outcome are we driving?
- How does this support business goals?
- What is the timeline?

Content assets needed:
- Hero content (main piece)
- Supporting content (related posts, emails)
- Promotional content (ads, social posts)
- Conversion content (landing pages, CTAs)

Distribution plan:
- Owned media (blog, email, social profiles)
- Earned media (PR, guest posts, influencers)
- Paid media (ads, sponsorships, promoted posts)

Campaign brief template:
- Campaign name
- Objective and success metrics
- Target audience segment
- Key messages
- Content pieces required
- Distribution channels
- Timeline
- Budget (if applicable)

</step_4>

</process>

<content_types>

<type name="blog-posts">
Purpose: SEO, education, authority
Length: 1,500-3,000 words for SEO
Frequency: 1-4 per month
Elements: Headlines, subheads, images, CTAs
</type>

<type name="social-posts">
Purpose: Engagement, awareness, traffic
Frequency: Daily to several times per week
Elements: Hook, value, CTA
</type>

<type name="email-newsletters">
Purpose: Nurture, traffic, sales
Frequency: Weekly or bi-weekly
Elements: Subject line, preview text, body, CTA
</type>

<type name="video-content">
Purpose: Education, trust-building, SEO (YouTube)
Length: 5-15 minutes for YouTube, 15-60 seconds for short-form
Elements: Hook (first 5 seconds), content, CTA
</type>

<type name="lead-magnets">
Purpose: List building, value demonstration
Format: PDF, video series, template, tool
Elements: Promise, content, next step
</type>

</content_types>
