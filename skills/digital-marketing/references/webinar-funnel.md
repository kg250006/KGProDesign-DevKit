<overview>
Webinar funnel structure for selling courses, programs, and high-ticket services through live or automated webinars.
</overview>

<funnel_structure>

<stage name="registration">
Goal: Get signups for webinar

Components:
- Registration page
- Confirmation page
- Reminder sequence

Key metric: Registration rate (target: 30-50% of page visitors)
</stage>

<stage name="attendance">
Goal: Get registrants to show up

Components:
- Reminder email sequence
- Calendar integration
- Pre-webinar content

Key metric: Show-up rate (target: 30-50% of registrants)
</stage>

<stage name="presentation">
Goal: Deliver value and make offer

Components:
- Webinar presentation
- Q&A session
- Pitch and offer

Key metric: Stay rate (target: 60-80% watch through pitch)
</stage>

<stage name="conversion">
Goal: Convert attendees to buyers

Components:
- Order page
- Replay sequence
- Follow-up emails

Key metric: Conversion rate (target: 5-15% of attendees)
</stage>

</funnel_structure>

<registration_page>

<elements>
Headline: Promise specific outcome from attending
"Free Training: How to [Achieve Specific Result] in [Timeframe]"

Subheadline: What they'll learn or discover
"Discover the 3-step system for..."

Bullet points: Specific takeaways (3-5)
- "The #1 mistake that keeps you stuck..."
- "The exact framework I used to..."
- "How to [result] without [common pain]..."

Host credibility: Why listen to you
- Photo
- Brief bio
- Key accomplishments

Registration form:
- Name and email
- Sometimes phone for SMS reminders
- Date/time selection (if multiple sessions)

Urgency: Limited spots or time
"Only 500 spots available" or "Reserve your spot now"
</elements>

<best_practices>
- Focus on one specific outcome
- Make the promise big but believable
- Show your face (builds trust)
- Keep form simple (2-3 fields max)
- Mobile-optimized
- Countdown timer optional
</best_practices>

</registration_page>

<reminder_sequence>

</email>

Subject: Starting in 1 hour - [Topic]
Content:
- Short and direct
- Access link prominently
- Excitement builder
</email>

<email name="reminder-3" timing="at-start">
Subject: We're LIVE - Join Now
Content:
- Starting now
- Direct link
- Quick reason to join
</email>

</reminder_sequence>

<webinar_structure>

<section name="hook" time="0-5 min">
Purpose: Capture attention, create anticipation

Elements:
- Big promise of what they'll learn
- Why this matters now
- Your credibility (brief)
- What they should do (stay till end, take notes)
</section>

<section name="story" time="5-15 min">
Purpose: Build connection and credibility

Elements:
- Your journey (struggle to success)
- The breakthrough moment
- What you discovered
- Why you're sharing it
</section>

<section name="content" time="15-45 min">
Purpose: Deliver value, teach framework

Structure:
- Teach 3 key principles/steps
- Give actionable insights
- Show examples and proof
- Keep them engaged with interaction

Teaching formula:
- What (the concept)
- Why (why it matters)
- How (general approach)
- Example (proof it works)
</section>

<section name="transition" time="45-50 min">
Purpose: Bridge from content to offer

Elements:
- Recap what they learned
- The gap (why this alone isn't enough)
- What's possible with complete solution
- Introduction to your offer
</section>

<section name="pitch" time="50-70 min">
Purpose: Present your offer compellingly

Elements:
- Product/program name and description
- Core modules/what's included
- Bonus 1 (value X)
- Bonus 2 (value Y)
- Bonus 3 (value Z)
- Total value stacking
- Investment (price)
- Guarantee
- Clear call to action
</section>

<section name="close" time="70-90 min">
Purpose: Handle objections, create urgency

Elements:
- FAQ/objection handling
- Testimonials and proof
- Urgency (deadline, limited spots)
- Final call to action
- Q&A
</section>

</webinar_structure>

</replay_sequence>

<live_vs_automated>

<option name="live">
Pros:
- Higher engagement and energy
- Real-time Q&A builds trust
- Urgency is genuine
- Better conversion rates typically

Cons:
- Time-intensive
- Schedule dependent
- Harder to scale

Best for: Premium offers, relationship-building, initial launches
</option>

<option name="automated">
Pros:
- Runs 24/7
- Scalable
- Consistent delivery
- Passive income potential

Cons:
- Lower perceived value
- No real interaction
- Ethics of fake "live" controversial

Best for: Proven webinars, lower-ticket offers, scale

Implementation:
- Record live webinar
- Use webinar platform with automation
- Simulate live elements
- Or be transparent ("On-demand training")
</option>

</live_vs_automated>

<metrics>

<metric name="registration-rate">
Formula: Registrations / Page visitors
Benchmark: 30-50%
Improve: Better headline, clearer value, simpler form
</metric>

<metric name="show-up-rate">
Formula: Attendees / Registrations
Benchmark: 30-50%
Improve: Better reminders, pre-webinar content, urgency
</metric>

<metric name="conversion-rate">
Formula: Buyers / Attendees
Benchmark: 5-15%
Improve: Better pitch, stronger offer, more proof
</metric>

<metric name="revenue-per-registrant">
Formula: Total revenue / Registrations
Track: Primary metric for scaling decisions
</metric>

</metrics>
