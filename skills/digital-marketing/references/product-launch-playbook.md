<overview>
Step-by-step product launch guide with timelines, tactics, and checklists for launching physical or digital products.
</overview>

<launch_phases>

<phase name="pre-launch" timeline="30-60 days before">

<task_group name="market-validation">
- Confirm product-market fit with customer research
- Validate pricing with pre-orders or surveys
- Gather early testimonials from beta users
- Document unique selling proposition
</task_group>

<task_group name="landing-page-creation">
- Create compelling headline focused on transformation
- Write benefit-driven copy (features → benefits → outcomes)
- Add social proof elements (testimonials, logos, numbers)
- Design clean, conversion-focused layout
- Set up email capture for waitlist
- Install analytics and tracking pixels
</task_group>

<task_group name="email-list-building">
- Create relevant lead magnet (related to product)
- Set up opt-in funnel with thank you page
- Build anticipation with "coming soon" messaging
- Run awareness ads to landing page
- Partner with complementary businesses for cross-promotion
</task_group>

<task_group name="social-proof-gathering">
- Collect beta user testimonials
- Create case studies with results
- Document before/after transformations
- Gather photos/videos of product in use
- Secure press mentions or reviews
</task_group>

<task_group name="content-asset-creation">
- Write sales page copy
- Create product images and videos
- Develop demo or walkthrough content
- Write email sequences (pre-launch, launch, post-launch)
- Prepare social media content calendar
- Create ad creative for paid campaigns
</task_group>

</phase>

<phase name="buzz-building" timeline="14-30 days before">

<task_group name="teaser-campaigns">
- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Reveal product features one at a time
- Run countdown campaigns
- Host "ask me anything" sessions
- Share early results or previews
</task_group>

<task_group name="influencer-outreach">
- Identify relevant influencers in your niche
- Send product samples for review
- Negotiate promotional partnerships
- Coordinate timing with influencer posts
- Provide affiliates with promotional assets
</task_group>

<task_group name="press-pr-activities">
- Write press release
- Create press kit with images and key facts
- Pitch to relevant media outlets
- Schedule podcast appearances
- Submit to Product Hunt (if applicable)
</task_group>

<task_group name="early-access-program">
- Offer exclusive early access to email list
- Create VIP or founding member tier
- Provide special bonuses for early buyers
- Build urgency with limited quantities
</task_group>

<task_group name="countdown-sequences">
- 14 days: Major announcement and preview
- 7 days: Detailed product reveal
- 3 days: Final countdown begins
- 1 day: Last chance to prepare
- Launch day: Gates open
</task_group>

</phase>

<phase name="launch-week" timeline="7-14 days">

<day_schedule>
Day 1 - Launch Day:
- Send launch email to full list
- Post on all social channels
- Activate paid advertising
- Monitor and respond to comments
- Share first sales wins

Day 2-3:
- Share behind-the-scenes of launch
- Highlight early customer reactions
- Address common questions
- Continue social engagement

Day 4-5:
- Feature case studies and testimonials
- Send "in case you missed it" email
- Handle objections in content
- Mid-launch stats update

Day 6-7:
- Introduce scarcity/urgency (if applicable)
- Send "only X days left" messaging
- Final push on all channels

Final Days:
- Last chance emails
- Countdown timers
- Final testimonials and results
- Cart close announcement
</day_schedule>

<launch_offers>
Incentives to drive urgency:
- Early bird pricing
- Limited quantity bonuses
- Exclusive founding member benefits
- Payment plan options
- Money-back guarantee
</launch_offers>

</phase>

<phase name="post-launch" timeline="30 days after">

<task_group name="momentum-maintenance">
- Continue engaging new customers
- Share ongoing success stories
- Answer questions and provide support
- Keep social media active with product content
</task_group>

<task_group name="feedback-collection">
- Survey new customers (NPS, satisfaction)
- Conduct post-purchase interviews
- Gather detailed testimonials
- Document improvement suggestions
- Track common support questions
</task_group>

<task_group name="iteration-planning">
- Analyze launch metrics
- Document what worked and what didn't
- Plan product improvements
- Consider evergreen launch transition
- Set up ongoing marketing systems
</task_group>

</phase>

</launch_phases>

<launch_checklists>

<checklist name="2-weeks-before">
- [ ] Sales page complete and tested
- [ ] Email sequences loaded and scheduled
- [ ] Payment system tested end-to-end
- [ ] Social content scheduled
- [ ] Ads created and approved
- [ ] Influencer partnerships confirmed
- [ ] Press outreach complete
- [ ] Team roles and responsibilities clear
</checklist>

<checklist name="1-week-before">
- [ ] Final review of all assets
- [ ] Test all links and forms
- [ ] Confirm all integrations working
- [ ] Brief team on launch day responsibilities
- [ ] Set up monitoring dashboards
- [ ] Prepare FAQ for support team
- [ ] Final email to waitlist building anticipation
</checklist>

<checklist name="launch-day">
- [ ] All automations activated
- [ ] Launch email sent
- [ ] Social posts published
- [ ] Ads activated
- [ ] Team monitoring assigned channels
- [ ] First sales confirmed and celebrated
- [ ] Issues addressed quickly
</checklist>

</launch_checklists>

<email_templates>

<template name="launch-announcement">
Subject: It's here: [Product Name] is now available

Body structure:
1. Big announcement (the wait is over)
2. What it is (brief description)
3. What it does for them (key benefit)
4. What's included (features/bonuses)
5. Special offer (early bird/bonuses)
6. Clear CTA (buy now button)
7. Urgency (limited time/quantity)
</template>

<template name="mid-launch-testimonial">
Subject: "[Result they achieved]" - [Customer name]

Body structure:
1. Customer quote or story
2. Their situation before
3. Their results after
4. Product connection
5. CTA to get same results
</template>

<template name="final-chance">
Subject: [X hours] left to get [Product Name]

Body structure:
1. Urgency (deadline is real)
2. Quick recap of value
3. What they'll miss
4. Final bonuses reminder
5. Strong CTA
6. PS with deadline
</template>

</email_templates>
