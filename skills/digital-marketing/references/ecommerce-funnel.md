<overview>
E-commerce funnel optimization for product-based businesses including acquisition, conversion, and retention.
</overview>

<funnel_structure>

<stage name="awareness">
Goal: Get discovered by potential customers

Channels:
- Organic social media
- SEO and content marketing
- Influencer partnerships
- Paid advertising
- Marketplace presence (Amazon, Etsy)
- PR and press coverage

Key metrics: Traffic, reach, impressions
</stage>

<stage name="consideration">
Goal: Get them interested in your products

Components:
- Product pages
- Category pages
- Blog content
- Comparison guides
- User-generated content
- Reviews and ratings

Key metrics: Pages per session, time on site
</stage>

<stage name="conversion">
Goal: Turn browsers into buyers

Components:
- Product pages (optimized)
- Cart experience
- Checkout flow
- Upsells and cross-sells
- Trust signals

Key metrics: Conversion rate, cart abandonment
</stage>

<stage name="retention">
Goal: Turn customers into repeat buyers

Components:
- Post-purchase emails
- Loyalty programs
- Subscription options
- Reorder reminders
- Personalized recommendations

Key metrics: Repeat purchase rate, LTV
</stage>

<stage name="advocacy">
Goal: Turn customers into promoters

Components:
- Review requests
- Referral programs
- User-generated content
- Social sharing
- Ambassador programs

Key metrics: Reviews, referrals, NPS
</stage>

</funnel_structure>

<product_page_optimization>

<element name="product-images">
Best practices:
- High-quality, multiple angles
- Lifestyle images showing use
- Zoom capability
- Video when possible
- User-submitted photos
- White background for clarity
</element>

<element name="product-title">
Best practices:
- Clear, descriptive
- Include key features/keywords
- Brand name (if relevant)
- Size/variant if applicable
</element>

<element name="price-presentation">
Best practices:
- Clear, prominent price
- Compare-at price for sales
- Savings highlighted
- Payment options (Afterpay, Klarna)
- Per-unit price if applicable
</element>

<element name="product-description">
Structure:
- Brief hook (1-2 sentences)
- Key benefits (bullet points)
- Detailed description
- Specifications
- Materials/ingredients
- Size guide if applicable
</element>

<element name="social-proof">
Types:
- Star rating prominently displayed
- Number of reviews
- Customer photos
- Testimonials
- "Bestseller" or "Most popular" badges
- Real-time purchase notifications
</element>

<element name="urgency-scarcity">
Tactics:
- Low stock warnings ("Only 3 left!")
- Limited time offers
- Countdown timers for sales
- Seasonal availability

Note: Only use if true
</element>

<element name="trust-signals">
Include:
- Secure checkout badge
- Money-back guarantee
- Free shipping threshold
- Return policy summary
- Payment method icons
</element>

<element name="call-to-action">
Best practices:
- Prominent "Add to Cart" button
- Contrasting color
- Above the fold
- Sticky on mobile
- Clear quantity selector
</element>

</product_page_optimization>

<cart_optimization>

<element name="cart-summary">
Clear display:
- Product image
- Name and variant
- Quantity selector
- Price
- Subtotal
- Easy remove option
</element>

<element name="shipping-info">
Transparency:
- Shipping cost shown early
- Free shipping threshold visible
- Estimated delivery date
- Shipping options
</element>

<element name="upsells-cross-sells">
Placement:
- "Frequently bought together"
- "Complete the look"
- "You might also like"
- Complementary products

Tips:
- Limit to 2-3 suggestions
- Relevant to cart contents
- Easy one-click add
</element>

<element name="trust-reassurance">
Reduce anxiety:
- Secure checkout badge
- Satisfaction guarantee
- Easy returns
- Customer support available
</element>

</cart_optimization>

<checkout_optimization>

<best_practices>
Speed and simplicity:
- Minimize required fields
- Guest checkout option
- Auto-fill when possible
- Progress indicator
- Mobile-optimized

Payment:
- Multiple payment options
- Express checkout (Apple Pay, Shop Pay)
- Payment plans (Afterpay, Klarna)
- Save payment for future

Trust:
- SSL certificate visible
- Trust badges
- Clear security messaging
- Order summary visible throughout

Shipping:
- Multiple options
- Clear delivery dates
- Free shipping threshold
- In-store pickup if applicable
</best_practices>

<cart_abandonment_recovery>
Email sequence:
- Email 1 (1 hour): Reminder of cart contents
- Email 2 (24 hours): Social proof + urgency
- Email 3 (48 hours): Incentive (discount if appropriate)

Other tactics:
- Exit intent popup
- SMS reminders
- Retargeting ads
- Browser push notifications
</cart_abandonment_recovery>

</checkout_optimization>

<email_sequences>

<sequence name="welcome">
For new subscribers (not yet customers):
- Email 1: Welcome + brand story + discount code
- Email 2: Popular products + customer reviews
- Email 3: How-to content or brand values
- Email 4: Final reminder of welcome offer
</sequence>

<sequence name="post-purchase">
After first purchase:
- Email 1 (Immediate): Order confirmation
- Email 2 (Shipped): Tracking info
- Email 3 (Delivered): How to use/care for product
- Email 4 (7 days): Request review
- Email 5 (14 days): Cross-sell recommendations
- Email 6 (30 days): Reorder reminder (if applicable)
</sequence>

<sequence name="win-back">
For lapsed customers (60-90 days no purchase):
- Email 1: "We miss you" + bestsellers
- Email 2: New arrivals
- Email 3: Incentive to return
</sequence>

<sequence name="browse-abandonment">
Viewed products but didn't add to cart:
- Email 1 (1 hour): "Still interested in [product]?"
- Email 2 (24 hours): Reviews and social proof
</sequence>

</email_sequences>

<retention_strategies>

<strategy name="loyalty-program">
Structure:
- Points per purchase
- Tier levels (Bronze, Silver, Gold)
- Exclusive perks
- Birthday rewards
- Early access to sales
</strategy>

<strategy name="subscription">
For consumable products:
- Subscribe and save discount
- Flexible frequency
- Easy pause/cancel
- Swap products
</strategy>

<strategy name="referral-program">
Structure:
- Give $X, Get $X
- Easy sharing (link, code)
- Track and reward
- Thank referrers
</strategy>

<strategy name="vip-treatment">
For best customers:
- Early access to new products
- Exclusive products
- Free shipping always
- Personal outreach
- Surprise gifts
</strategy>

</retention_strategies>

<key_metrics>

<metric name="conversion-rate">
Formula: Orders / Sessions
Benchmark: 1-3% (varies by industry)
</metric>

<metric name="average-order-value">
Formula: Revenue / Orders
Improve: Upsells, bundles, free shipping threshold
</metric>

<metric name="cart-abandonment-rate">
Formula: Carts abandoned / Carts created
Benchmark: 70% average (work to reduce)
</metric>

<metric name="customer-lifetime-value">
Formula: AOV x Purchase frequency x Customer lifespan
Goal: 3x customer acquisition cost
</metric>

<metric name="repeat-purchase-rate">
Formula: Customers with 2+ orders / Total customers
Benchmark: 20-30%
</metric>

</key_metrics>
