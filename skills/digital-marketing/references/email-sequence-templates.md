<overview>
Email copy templates and frameworks for welcome sequences, sales sequences, and nurture campaigns.
</overview>

<email_formulas>

<formula name="aida">
Attention: Hook them with subject line and opening
Interest: Build curiosity with relevant content
Desire: Create want with benefits and proof
Action: Tell them exactly what to do next
</formula>

<formula name="pas">
Problem: Agitate the pain point
Agitation: Make it worse, show consequences
Solution: Present your answer
</formula>

<formula name="bab">
Before: Their current situation (the struggle)
After: Their ideal situation (the dream)
Bridge: How you get them there
</formula>

<formula name="4ps">
Picture: Paint the outcome
Promise: What you guarantee
Proof: Evidence it works
Push: Call to action
</formula>

</email_formulas>
