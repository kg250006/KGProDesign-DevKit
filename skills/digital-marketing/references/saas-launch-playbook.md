<overview>
SaaS and software product launch approach covering beta programs, early access, Product Hunt, and growth strategies.
</overview>

<saas_launch_phases>

<phase name="private-beta" timeline="2-3 months before public launch">

<task_group name="waitlist-building">
Build anticipation:
- Create landing page with value proposition
- Collect emails with waitlist signup
- Offer early access incentives
- Run targeted ads to ideal users
- Leverage network for initial signups

Waitlist content:
- Problem you're solving
- Solution preview
- Expected timeline
- Early access benefits
- Social proof (team, investors, advisors)
</task_group>

<task_group name="beta-user-recruitment">
Find right testers:
- 10-50 users for private beta
- Mix of target customer segments
- Variety of technical abilities
- Engaged and willing to give feedback
- Ideally, users with urgent need

Sources:
- Personal network
- Waitlist most engaged subscribers
- Industry communities
- LinkedIn outreach
- Startup communities
</task_group>

<task_group name="feedback-loops">
Systematic learning:
- Onboarding surveys
- Weekly feedback calls
- In-app feedback tools
- Usage analytics tracking
- Bug reporting system
- NPS surveys

Key questions:
- What's confusing?
- What's missing?
- What's most valuable?
- Would you recommend us?
- How would you describe us?
</task_group>

<task_group name="feature-iteration">
Rapid improvement:
- Prioritize feedback by frequency and impact
- Ship improvements weekly
- Communicate updates to beta users
- Validate changes work before scaling
- Document learnings for launch
</task_group>

</phase>

<phase name="early-access" timeline="1-2 months before public launch">

<task_group name="limited-access-program">
Controlled expansion:
- Open to 50-200 users
- Still invite-only or application
- Founder pricing / lifetime deals
- White-glove onboarding
- Case study development

Benefits for early users:
- Discounted pricing locked forever
- Direct access to founders
- Input on product roadmap
- Exclusive community
- Founding member status
</task_group>

<task_group name="founder-pricing">
Incentivize early adoption:
- 40-70% off regular pricing
- Locked for lifetime of account
- Limited availability (creates urgency)
- Requires commitment (annual payment)

Example tiers:
- Lifetime deal: One-time payment for unlimited access
- Founder annual: 50% off locked forever
- Early adopter: 30% off first year
</task_group>

<task_group name="case-study-development">
Build social proof:
- Identify most successful early users
- Document their before/after results
- Create detailed case studies
- Gather video testimonials
- Quantify ROI where possible
</task_group>

<task_group name="product-validation">
Confirm market fit:
- Users willing to pay (conversion)
- Users continuing to pay (retention)
- Users recommending (NPS > 40)
- Users active (engagement metrics)
- Clear use cases emerging
</task_group>

</phase>

<phase name="public-launch" timeline="launch week">

<task_group name="product-hunt-launch">
Maximize visibility:
- Choose optimal day (Tuesday-Thursday)
- Prepare all assets in advance
- Coordinate team for engagement
- Reach out to hunter (if applicable)
- Prepare for traffic surge

Product Hunt assets:
- Compelling tagline (60 chars)
- Description (260 chars)
- Gallery images/GIF
- Maker comment ready
- First comment strategy

Launch day:
- Launch at 12:01 AM PT
- Engage with every comment
- Share across social channels
- Email to list for support
- Monitor and respond all day
</task_group>

<task_group name="press-media-coverage">
Amplify launch:
- Press release to relevant outlets
- Pitch to tech journalists
- Guest posts on industry blogs
- Podcast interviews scheduled
- Social media announcements
</task_group>

<task_group name="content-marketing">
Capture organic interest:
- Launch blog post
- Comparison posts (vs alternatives)
- How-to guides
- Integration announcements
- Founder story content
</task_group>

<task_group name="paid-acquisition">
Scale reach:
- Google Ads for high-intent keywords
- LinkedIn for B2B audiences
- Facebook/Instagram for broader reach
- Retargeting for website visitors
- Start small, optimize, then scale
</task_group>

</phase>

<phase name="growth" timeline="post-launch ongoing">

<task_group name="user-onboarding-optimization">
Reduce time to value:
- Streamlined signup flow
- In-app onboarding guides
- Progress indicators
- Activation milestones
- Proactive support triggers

Key metrics:
- Signup to activation rate
- Time to first value
- Feature adoption rates
- Support ticket volume
</task_group>

<task_group name="retention-metrics">
Keep users engaged:
- Track key engagement actions
- Identify churn predictors
- Build engagement loops
- Create habit-forming features
- Monitor cohort retention

Key metrics:
- DAU/MAU ratio
- Retention by cohort
- Feature usage frequency
- Churn rate
- Expansion revenue
</task_group>

<task_group name="expansion-revenue">
Grow existing accounts:
- Usage-based pricing tiers
- Seat-based expansion
- Add-on features
- Professional services
- Annual prepay incentives
</task_group>

<task_group name="referral-programs">
Turn users into advocates:
- In-product referral prompts
- Referral incentives (credit, months free)
- Affiliate program for influencers
- Case study program
- User community building
</task_group>

</phase>

</saas_launch_phases>

<saas_specific_tactics>

<tactic name="freemium-vs-trial">
Freemium:
- Free tier with limited features forever
- Works when many free users convert
- Great for viral/network products
- Higher volume, lower conversion

Free trial:
- Full access for limited time (7-14 days)
- Works when product value is clear quickly
- Higher conversion rate
- Requires faster time to value
</tactic>

<tactic name="product-led-growth">
Let product drive acquisition:
- Self-serve signup and onboarding
- Viral/shareable features
- In-product upgrade prompts
- Usage-based expansion
- Minimal sales touch needed
</tactic>

<tactic name="developer-relations">
For developer-focused products:
- Great documentation
- Active on Stack Overflow
- Open source components
- Developer community building
- Hackathons and meetups
</tactic>

<tactic name="integration-ecosystem">
Increase stickiness:
- Connect to tools users already use
- Build integration marketplace
- Partner with complementary products
- API for custom integrations
</tactic>

</saas_specific_tactics>
