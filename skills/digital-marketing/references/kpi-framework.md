<overview>
Key Performance Indicator framework for measuring marketing success across channels and funnel stages.
</overview>

<framework>

<section name="goal-alignment">

Start with business goals:
1. What is the primary business objective?
2. What marketing outcome supports that objective?
3. What metrics indicate progress toward that outcome?

Example cascade:
- Business goal: $500K annual revenue
- Marketing objective: Generate 500 qualified leads per month
- Key metrics: Traffic, opt-in rate, lead quality, conversion rate
</section>

<section name="metric-hierarchy">

<level name="north-star">
Single most important metric that indicates overall success.
Examples:
- Revenue attributed to marketing
- Number of customers acquired
- Monthly recurring revenue
</level>

<level name="primary-kpis">
3-5 metrics that directly drive the north star.
Examples:
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV)
- Conversion rate
- Average order value
</level>

<level name="supporting-metrics">
Metrics that inform primary KPIs but aren't the main focus.
Examples:
- Traffic volume
- Email open rates
- Social engagement
- Time on site
</level>

<level name="diagnostic-metrics">
Metrics used to troubleshoot when primary KPIs are off.
Examples:
- Bounce rate by traffic source
- Click-through by email segment
- Conversion by device type
</level>

</section>

</framework>

<metrics_by_channel>

<channel name="website">
Traffic Metrics:
- Sessions: Total visits to site
- Users: Unique visitors
- Pageviews: Pages viewed
- Pages per session: Engagement depth
- Avg. session duration: Time spent
- Bounce rate: Single-page visits (lower is better)

Conversion Metrics:
- Conversion rate: Visitors who complete goal
- Goal completions: Number of conversions
- Conversion value: Revenue from conversions
</channel>

<channel name="email">
Delivery Metrics:
- Delivery rate: Emails delivered / sent
- Bounce rate: Emails bounced / sent

Engagement Metrics:
- Open rate: Opens / delivered
- Click rate: Clicks / delivered
- Click-to-open rate: Clicks / opens
- Unsubscribe rate: Unsubscribes / delivered

Revenue Metrics:
- Revenue per email: Total revenue / emails sent
- Revenue per subscriber: Total revenue / list size
- Conversion rate: Purchases / clicks
</channel>

<channel name="social-media">
Reach Metrics:
- Followers/Fans: Audience size
- Reach: Unique accounts who saw content
- Impressions: Total times content displayed

Engagement Metrics:
- Engagement rate: (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
- Likes/Reactions: Basic engagement
- Comments: Deeper engagement
- Shares: Viral potential
- Saves: Content value (Instagram)

Traffic Metrics:
- Link clicks: Visits to website
- Click-through rate: Clicks / impressions
</channel>

<channel name="paid-advertising">
Reach Metrics:
- Impressions: Ad displays
- Reach: Unique people who saw ad
- Frequency: Average times seen per person

Cost Metrics:
- CPM: Cost per 1,000 impressions
- CPC: Cost per click
- CPA: Cost per acquisition
- CAC: Customer acquisition cost

Performance Metrics:
- CTR: Click-through rate (clicks / impressions)
- Conversion rate: Conversions / clicks
- ROAS: Return on ad spend (revenue / cost)
</channel>

<channel name="seo">
Ranking Metrics:
- Keyword positions: Where you rank
- Keywords ranking: Total keywords in top 10/100
- Position changes: Movement over time

Traffic Metrics:
- Organic sessions: Visits from search
- Organic users: Unique visitors from search
- Organic CTR: Clicks / impressions in search

Engagement Metrics:
- Organic bounce rate: Engagement from search visitors
- Organic conversion rate: Conversions from search
</channel>

</metrics_by_channel>

<metrics_by_funnel_stage>

<stage name="awareness">
Metrics:
- Reach / Impressions
- Website traffic
- Social followers
- Video views
- Brand mentions

Goal: Maximize relevant exposure
</stage>

<stage name="interest">
Metrics:
- Time on site
- Pages per session
- Blog engagement
- Email subscribers
- Social engagement

Goal: Capture and hold attention
</stage>

<stage name="consideration">
Metrics:
- Lead magnet downloads
- Webinar registrations
- Demo requests
- Email click rates
- Return visitors

Goal: Move from casual to serious interest
</stage>

<stage name="conversion">
Metrics:
- Conversion rate
- Sales
- Revenue
- Average order value
- Cost per acquisition

Goal: Turn interest into action
</stage>

<stage name="retention">
Metrics:
- Repeat purchase rate
- Customer lifetime value
- Churn rate
- NPS score
- Engagement with post-purchase content

Goal: Maximize customer value
</stage>

<stage name="advocacy">
Metrics:
- Referrals
- Reviews and ratings
- Social shares
- Testimonial submissions
- User-generated content

Goal: Turn customers into promoters
</stage>

</metrics_by_funnel_stage>

<dashboard_setup>

<weekly_dashboard>
Quick health check metrics:
- Revenue vs. goal
- Traffic vs. previous week
- Email performance (sends, opens, clicks)
- Social engagement
- Ad spend and ROAS
- Key conversion rates
</weekly_dashboard>

<monthly_dashboard>
Strategic metrics:
- Revenue and trend
- Traffic by source and trend
- Lead generation and quality
- Customer acquisition cost
- Email list growth
- Social following growth
- SEO keyword rankings
- Month-over-month comparisons
</monthly_dashboard>

<quarterly_review>
Strategic analysis:
- Progress toward annual goals
- Channel performance comparison
- Customer lifetime value trends
- Marketing ROI by channel
- Campaign performance summary
- Competitive position changes
- Strategic adjustments needed
</quarterly_review>

</dashboard_setup>
