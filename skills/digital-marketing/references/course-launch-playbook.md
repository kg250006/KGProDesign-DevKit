<overview>
Digital course and program launch strategy covering live launches, enrollment windows, and evergreen transitions.
</overview>

<course_launch_models>

<model name="live-launch">
Characteristics:
- Fixed enrollment windows (open 5-10 days)
- Live events (webinars, challenges)
- Real scarcity and urgency
- Cohort-based learning
- Higher engagement and completion

Best for:
- Premium courses ($500+)
- Community-based programs
- Courses with live elements
- Building initial momentum
- Testing course concepts

Typical results:
- Higher conversion rates (5-15%)
- Larger average order value
- More testimonials and engagement
- Concentrated revenue bursts
</model>

<model name="evergreen">
Characteristics:
- Always-open enrollment
- Automated webinar funnels
- Drip content delivery
- Self-paced learning
- Consistent, predictable revenue

Best for:
- Validated courses with proof
- Lower-priced courses ($50-500)
- Hands-off income goals
- Scaling beyond live capacity
- Passive income strategy

Typical results:
- Lower conversion rates (1-5%)
- Consistent monthly revenue
- Less intensive to maintain
- Scalable with ads
</model>

<model name="hybrid">
Characteristics:
- Evergreen front-end (lead gen)
- Periodic live launches (sales push)
- Best of both worlds
- Multiple revenue streams

Best for:
- Established course creators
- Products with seasonal demand
- Maximizing revenue potential
</model>

</course_launch_models>

<launch_phases>

<phase name="pre-launch-list-building" timeline="30-90 days before">

<task_group name="free-training-workshop">
Value-first content:
- Free webinar or video series
- Live challenge (5-7 days)
- Mini-course (email-based)
- Workshop or masterclass

Content structure:
- Teach valuable standalone concept
- Demonstrate expertise and methodology
- Create desire for complete solution
- Naturally lead to paid offer
</task_group>

<task_group name="lead-magnet">
Entry point offers:
- Related to course topic
- Quick win delivery
- Demonstrates transformation
- Qualifies right buyers

Examples:
- Cheat sheet or checklist
- Template or swipe file
- Assessment or quiz
- Chapter or module preview
</task_group>

<task_group name="challenge-or-mini-course">
Engagement builders:
- 5-7 day challenge format
- Daily emails with tasks
- Community component (Facebook group)
- Small wins that build desire
- Primes audience for main offer
</task_group>

</phase>

<phase name="launch-sales-event" timeline="5-10 days">

<task_group name="live-webinar">
Webinar structure (60-90 min):
- Hook (0-5 min): Capture attention with big promise
- Story (5-15 min): Share your journey and credibility
- Content (20-40 min): Teach valuable framework
- Transition (5 min): Bridge to offer
- Pitch (15-20 min): Present course details
- Close (10 min): Handle objections, create urgency
- Q&A (10-15 min): Address questions live

Webinar optimization:
- Compelling title with specific promise
- Registration page with bullet benefits
- Multiple reminder emails
- Engagement during webinar (polls, chat)
- Replay strategy for non-attendees
</task_group>

<task_group name="email-launch-sequence">
Pre-launch emails:
- 14 days: Story and problem awareness
- 7 days: Solution preview and anticipation
- 3 days: Webinar/launch event reminder
- 1 day: Final reminder

Launch emails:
- Day 1: Doors open announcement
- Day 2: Feature/benefit focus
- Day 3: Testimonial/case study
- Day 4: FAQ/objection handling
- Day 5: Bonus stack reminder
- Day 6-7: Urgency/deadline
- Final hours: Last chance
</task_group>

<task_group name="bonus-stacking">
Increase perceived value:
- Fast-action bonuses (first 24-48 hours)
- Core bonuses (included for all)
- Expiring bonuses (removed at deadline)

Bonus types:
- Additional training modules
- Templates and swipe files
- 1:1 calls or coaching
- Community access
- Tools or software
- Physical products
</task_group>

<task_group name="deadline-urgency">
Create real scarcity:
- Cart close at specific date/time
- Price increase after launch
- Bonus removal deadline
- Limited seats (if true)

Deadline messaging:
- Countdown timers on pages
- Email subject lines with time
- Final day/hour reminders
- Authentic urgency language
</task_group>

</phase>

<phase name="post-launch-delivery" timeline="ongoing">

<task_group name="student-onboarding">
Welcome sequence:
- Access instructions email
- Quick start guide
- Community orientation
- First week expectations
- Celebration of decision
</task_group>

<task_group name="community-engagement">
Drive participation:
- Welcome new members
- Daily/weekly prompts
- Live Q&A sessions
- Student spotlights
- Accountability structures
</task_group>

<task_group name="testimonial-collection">
Gather social proof:
- Week 1: Quick win testimonials
- Week 3-4: Progress testimonials
- Completion: Transformation testimonials
- Ongoing: Results-based testimonials

Collection methods:
- Direct outreach to engaged students
- Post-module feedback requests
- Celebration posts in community
- Video testimonial requests
</task_group>

</phase>

</launch_phases>

Email 2: Quick start + first module

Email 4: What to expect this week
Email 5: First win celebration prompt
Email 6: Engagement check-in

<webinar_script_outline>

<section name="hook" time="0-5 min">
- Big promise headline
- Who this is for
- What they'll learn
- Why pay attention
</section>

<section name="story" time="5-15 min">
- Your background and credibility
- Your struggle (relatable)
- Your breakthrough moment
- What changed everything
</section>

<section name="content" time="15-45 min">
- Framework introduction
- Step/pillar 1 (teach and example)
- Step/pillar 2 (teach and example)
- Step/pillar 3 (teach and example)
- Quick wins they can implement
</section>

<section name="transition" time="45-50 min">
- Recap the framework
- Show the gap (DIY limitations)
- Introduce the complete solution
</section>

<section name="pitch" time="50-70 min">
- Product name and description
- Core modules/curriculum
- Bonus 1, 2, 3...
- Total value vs price
- Guarantee
- Clear CTA
</section>

<section name="close" time="70-90 min">
- Urgency and scarcity
- Common objections addressed
- Final CTA
- Q&A
</section>

</webinar_script_outline>
